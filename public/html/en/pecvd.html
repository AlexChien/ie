Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) System

About PECVD

Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) is an excellent method for depositing a variety of thin films at lower temperatures and low pressure. It uses electrical energy to generate a glow discharge (plasma) in which the energy is transferred into a gas mixture. This transforms the gas mixture into reactive radicals, ions, neutral atoms and molecules, and other highly excited species. These atomic and molecular fragments interact with a substrate and deposit films on the substrate. In the field of Si thin film solar cells, PECVD mainly is used to deposit Si thin films which act as the photovoltaic conversion layers.

Ideal Energy PECVD System

About Ideal Energy PECVD System

Fig 1 Ideal Energy PECVD System

Ideal Energy’s PECVD System is consisted of five chambers which are one Load Lock In (LLI) chamber, one Load Lock Out (LLO) chamber and 3 Process Module (PM) chambers. The first PECVD system was successfully launched in December 2011 with all passed functional tests by customer. (Ideal Energy’s PECVD system picture shown as Fig.1)

Features of Ideal Energy PECVD System
  • For large surface area thin film solar cells and specially designed for the 5th generation size (1.1m x 1.3m);
  • Depositing tandem-junction (a-Si/μc-Si) solar cells in one tool (Three PM chambers: one for amorphous layer and other two for microcrystalline layers) without breaking vacuum to ensure highest level of process control with less heating and cooling steps;
  • Advanced double-layer vacuum design for all three PM chambers. Each vacuum PM chamber has several self-contained deposition boxes which assure the consistency for each box’s deposition.
  • Independent LLI & LLO chamber: Pre-heating LLI chamber and cooling in LLO chamber improve the pace of production; room temperature glass load-in and load-out minimize the surface contamination of glass and guarantee the operation safety.
  • Robot in Vacuum system: compared with the system that has carrier system, our design avoids cross contamination
  • Special design to eliminate cross contamination: the design eliminates the cross contamination between p-layer and i-layer, which can be finished in one chamber instead of in two chambers. This design reduces unnecessary transfer steps and accelerates the production pace too.;
  • Large area, VHF technology route: IE is the first company in China using VHF RF technology for thin film solar technology. We have first-class RF expert team, based on our many years semiconductor experience, profound theoretical and experimental expertise, we successfully solved the VHF feeding problem and standing wave effect and finally improve the deposition rate of Si thin films;
  • Stability and reliability: by introducing advanced semiconductor testing procedure, all our equipment parts and the whole system have been tested completely, and successfully passed the strictly testing requirements.
  • Long maintenance cycle: Using PRS clean mothod to decrease the clean time and improve the pace of production.
  • Advanced software controlling system: self-developed software and controlling software package; interfaces friendly, operation easily and data collection conveniently.
