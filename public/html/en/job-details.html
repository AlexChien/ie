Position: Cost Accounting Supervisor职位：成本主管
Specific responsibilities（岗位职责）
  • Key member in supporting the upcoming corporate ERP implementation in the general/ cost accounting modules and systems by developing effective and accurate costing accounting process and procedures
  • 支持公司即将实施的常规采购，成本会计模块和系统，发展有效和精确的成本计算工艺和程序
  • Reconcile and analyze inventory, variances, mfg labor & overhead, and closely supporting production and material supply operation
  • 调和及分析库存、变动、生产部人工和日常开支，支持产品和材料供应操作
  • Analyze and make proper judgment in accruing installation and warranty, E&O, and scrap liabilities
  • 分析并对安装和保修、错误排查以及报废品作出适当的判断
  • Prepare all COGS related journal entries and be part of monthly and quarterly closing and analysis
  • 整理与所有分类账目有关的销售成本，作为每月和每季的结算与分析
  • Accurately track project costing and make cost reduction proposals
  • 精确追踪项目花销，作出成本降低的提案
Requirements (任职资格)
  • Bachelor in accounting
  • 本科会计学位
  • 5 year plus manufacturing company costing experiences a must, preferably in hi-tech equipment company
  • 必须具有五年以上制造业公司成本管理经验，在高科技设备公司尤佳
  • Experienced in inventory management, inventory count, fixed asset
  • 在库存管理，库存统计，固定资产方面有经验
  • Excellent Excel and other database skills
  • 出色的Excel和其他数据库技能
  • SAP, Oracle or other ERP implementation experiences highly desirable
  • SAP，Oracle和其他ERP实施经验者优先
  • Hard working and capable of multi-task
  • 吃苦耐劳，能够同时处理多件事
  • Great team player with pleasant personality
  • 团队精神
  • Self-starter, strong attention to details and well organized
  • 工作主动，对细节充分把控，有组织有纪律
  • Strong verbal and written communication, analytical and decision-making skills
  • 出色的口语和书面沟通能力，分析和决策能力
  • Enjoy working for fast growing hi-tech venture
  • 乐于从事高速发展的高科技先驱工作
